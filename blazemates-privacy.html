BlazeMates is a social pairing app for adults 21+ who want to connect for cannabis-friendly hangs. This notice explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password are collected by our authentication provider (Supabase) so we can create and secure your account.
  • Profile details you share: Name, age, bio, preferred strain, blaze style, what you are looking for, and other profile fields you fill out or edit in the app.
  • Photos: Profile images you upload, capture with the in-app camera, or store as pending uploads.
  • Match and chat content: Messages you send, threads that are created, and match interactions so we can show your conversations and mutual connections.
  • Device storage caches: The mobile app stores copies of your session tokens and profile information in AsyncStorage so you stay signed in and your profile loads quickly offline.
  • Location access: If you enable location permissions we only use your device location to display the map of nearby hotspots. We do not store your precise location on our servers.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate you, keep your session active, and protect your account.
  • Build your profile, show it to other users, and surface compatible matches.
  • Power in-app chat, matches, and avatar storage.
  • Sync profile updates across devices and let you manage your information.
  • Improve reliability and safety, detect abuse, and support customer requests.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run BlazeMates:

  • Supabase: Provides authentication, database, and storage hosting. Supabase stores your account credentials, profile data, chats, and profile media on our behalf.
  • Expo platform services: The mobile app relies on the Expo runtime to request device permissions (camera, photos, location) and deliver updates. Expo does not receive your profile content from us.

We may disclose information if required by law, to enforce our policies, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BlazeMates users or others.

Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your account data while you maintain a BlazeMates profile. You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Delete Account. This triggers our Supabase edge function to remove your authentication record, messages, profile row, and stored avatar. You can also email us to request deletion. We may retain limited data if required for legal or security reasons.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Edit your profile details and photos directly in the app.
  • Manage device permissions (camera, photos, location) through your OS settings.
  • Sign out to clear the local cache or uninstall the app at any time.
  • Request account deletion or ask questions by contacting us.

Security

We rely on Supabase security controls, including encrypted connections, hashed passwords, and row-level security for database tables. No system is perfectly secure, so please use a strong password and contact us if you suspect unauthorized activity.

Children & Age Requirements

BlazeMates is for adults 21 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21. If we learn that we have information about someone under 21, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. If we make material changes we will notify you in the app or by other appropriate means. Continued use of BlazeMates after the update becomes effective means you accept the revised policy.
