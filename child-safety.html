BlazeMates Child Safety Standards

Effective date: 2025-08-01

Our Commitment

BlazeMates is committed to keeping every user safe and does not tolerate any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment.

Age Verification

BlazeMates is restricted to adults aged 21 and older. The app enforces an in-app age gate and is configured in Google Play Console to block minors from installing or signing into the service, in accordance with the Age-Restricted Content and Functionality policy.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

  • No sexual content, imagery, or conversation involving anyone under 18 years old.
  • No solicitation, grooming, trafficking, or exploitation of minors.
  • No sharing of sexual images of minors, including self-generated content.

Enforcement

  • We immediately remove any violating content and escalate it to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations and preserve evidence as required.

Reporting

If you see or suspect any behavior that violates these standards, report it immediately through the in-app reporting tools or email us at hawkcade21@gmail.com. Law enforcement or hotlines may also be contacted directly when urgent assistance is required.
