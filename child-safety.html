BlazeMates Child Safety Standards

Effective date: 2025-08-01

Our Commitment

BlazeMates is committed to ensuring the safety of all users and does not tolerate any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

  • No sexual content or behavior involving anyone under 18 years old.
  • No solicitation, grooming, or exploitation of minors.
  • No sharing of any sexual images of minors, including self-generated content.

Enforcement

  • We immediately remove any violating content and report it to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.
